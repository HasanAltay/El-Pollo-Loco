How to Play

Get ready to run, jump, and salsa your way through an epic adventure with Pepe! As you make your way through the game, watch out for those feisty chickens that will stop at nothing to take you down. Whether you're dodging dangerous chickens or taking them out with a well-timed salsa bottle, the action never stops. And don't forget to keep an eye out for those sneaky little chicks that will jump out of nowhere and trip you up. But it's all worth it for the ultimate showdown with the boss chicken. Give him a taste of your spiciest salsa and show him who's boss.
